Computer Vision

Think about how you could use advanced learning systems along with the camera on a user's phone to detect interesting characteristics about a piece of clothing of an outfit that the user is pointing at. Maybe help search similar items, or detect some features about the product!

Big Data

Storage became cheap and everyone now collects large amounts of data. But can you apply your favourite machine learning algorithm to find new meaning in the data? Could you find patterns in a user's buying behaviour? Maybe there is a piece of fashion that data says should exist, but currently does not?

Artificial Intelligence

It is now possible to build systems that understand human language, both written and speech. Could the job of a physical shopkeeper be now replaced with a virtual one? Could your next stylist be an app on your phone? Could calling customer support be a thing of the past?

Photos - Hackathon 2015

The mayhem, the madness, the celebration.

Hackerramp 2015 was Myntra’s first open hackathon.

Thanks to all the 180 participants who made this event a grand success. We had around 35 teams presenting their amazing hacks to the Jury.

The goal was simple - make the next big thing in mobile. Be it an app, an API or even something related to mobile security.

For more than 24 hours, keeping themselves fuelled on Red Bull, the teams raced the clock and converted their ideas into real working demos.

There was great food throughout the day, great talks to kick off the event and some great goodies for the participants to take home!

The Winners from the 2015 Hackathon

When you have 35 highly-driven teams competing for a prize bucket of 3,00,000 rupees, you know they Jury is going to have a hard time deciding. So hard, that they actually announced four winners!

  1. Chat based commerce (Daredevils): Himadri, Jatin, Vijay and Mohan from Hike. They are part of Hike’s iOS team. Prize: Rs. 1,50,000.
  2. Chat with friends realtime to buy products (Fashion Friends): Nikhil Bansal, Anurag Saxena, Shivam Gupta and Neeraj Singh. They are from Parcelled, Marketshare, Vizury and Flipkart. Prize: Rs. 75,000.
  3. Augmented Reality Shooting Game (Massacre): Nilesh Hiray and Naveen Reddy from Myntra. Prize: Rs. 50,000.
  4. Smart Links (The app that links all other apps): Niketh and Niteesh. They are from Yahoo and Fungru. Prize: Rs. 25,000.
